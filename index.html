About

My name is Behnam Nikkhah and I'm a fresh enthusiast in digital design and website development.

I've always wanted to make my own website, but to do that, I needed to learn more about website development to get started. A website should be treated like any other product. A product needs to have a good design, layout, and accessibility in order for the product to be usable and enjoyable. And a website is no exception.

As a Computer Science university graduate, I've come with a lot of knowledge about software development, but lacked the knowledge on good design practices. However, with the help of accredited Codecademy PRO INTENSIVE (premium) courses, I have gained much insight upon front and back-end development. To complement these courses, I've also taken a design course which focusd on the importance of colors, accessibility, and responsive design.
